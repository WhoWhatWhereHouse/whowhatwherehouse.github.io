Thugshells — Privacy Policy

Effective 11 July 2026

Thugshells is a free fan app for the freestyle rapper & DJ Thug Shells. We keep it simple: the app collects as little as possible, has no ads, and never sells your data. This policy explains exactly what it does.

What the app collects

  • Push notification token — only if you turn on Alerts. To deliver notifications we use Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google), which assigns your install a pseudonymous device token and the topics you opted into. It is not linked to your name or identity.
  • Your alert choices — the on/off state of each alert is stored only on your device.
  • Basic technical data — when the app loads its content (quotes, schedule, events), your device makes a standard web request; our content host and its CDN can see your IP address, as with any website.

The app does not collect your name, email, location, contacts, or any usage/analytics data, and contains no advertising or third-party trackers.

Newsletter

If you tap “Join the newsletter,” the app opens an external sign-up page hosted by our email provider. Your email address is provided to and handled by that provider under their own privacy policy — the app never sees or stores it. You can unsubscribe any time from the link in their emails.

How we use it

Solely to deliver the alerts you opt into and to show current content. Nothing more.

Sharing

Your push token is shared with Google (Firebase Cloud Messaging) only to route notifications — see Firebase’s privacy information and Google’s Privacy Policy. Content requests go to our host. We do not sell or share your data for advertising, and we do not track you across apps or websites.

Your choices

  • Turn any alert off in the app at any time — that unsubscribes you from the matching notifications.
  • Revoke notification permission in your device’s system settings.
  • Uninstalling the app removes the on-device data it stored.

Children

Thugshells is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes

If this policy changes, we’ll update this page and its effective date.

Contact

Questions? Reach us through the support page or any of Thug Shells’ social channels.
